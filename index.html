Features Available in The Watch

Notification alert

Notifications in our smartwatch are delivered via a synchronized connection with a paired smartphone or through an internet connection.

live chat

Live chats on our smartwatch typically function as an extension of a paired smartphone app. Users receive message notifications, can view and reply to.

heart beat tracker

The heart rate tracker in a smartwatch uses optical sensors to continuously monitor changes in blood flow beneath the skin, typically at the wrist.

GPS tracker

The GPS tracker in a smartwatch relies on signals from satellites to pinpoint the device's location. It uses these signals to calculate precise coordinates.

wifi facility

A smartwatch can connect to Wi-Fi networks by using its built-in Wi-Fi chip. It scans for available Wi-Fi networks, and when a network is selected.

weather updates

Weather updates on a smartwatch are typically provided through a connected smartphone or a built-in internet connection.
